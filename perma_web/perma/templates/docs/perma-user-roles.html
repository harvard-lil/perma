Perma account-holders fall into three categories: (1) Registrar Users; (2) Vesting Users; and (3) General Users. Each of these categories carries different authorities and responsibilities.

Registrar User

Registrars are institutions (typically libraries) that create and support the activities of Vesting Organizations. Registrars act through Registrar Users - individual account-holders authorized to act on behalf of the institution for purposes of Perma. Registrar Users have the authority to create new Vesting Organizations and Vesting Users and to vest Perma Links.


Vesting User

Vesting Organizations are entities (typically journals or courts) that administer the vesting of Perma Links relevant to them. Vesting Organizations act through Vesting Users - individual account-holders authorized to vest Perma Links on behalf of the Vesting Organization and to create new Vesting Users affiliated with the Vesting Organization.


General User

General users can create and manage their own Perma Links and temporary archives, but they do not have vesting authority.
