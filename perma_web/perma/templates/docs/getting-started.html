Creating an Account

To use Perma to archive web pages, you need a Perma account. To create an account, click here or find Create An Account on the home page

All we need is your email, first and last name. Please read the terms of service!


Your Perma Dashboard

When you log in to Perma, you’ll see your Perma “Dashboard.” You also can access your dashboard anytime by clicking “Dashboard” in the upper right corner of your screen.

From your Dashboard, you can create new Perma Links, view your existing Perma Links and archives, and access your account settings.
