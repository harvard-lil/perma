Libraries power Perma.cc

For centuries, libraries have worked together to preserve print sources and to make those sources accessible to the public. Today they do the same for online sources through Perma’s active network of library partners.

How Perma.cc works for libraries

We call our library partners “registrars,” and our network of registrars is now {{registrar_count}} institutions strong. Each registrar sponsors and administers Perma for its local community of scholars and journals.

Check out our user guide to learn more about how libraries use Perma.cc.

Join our library network

Libraries join Perma.cc to make Perma.cc available to their patrons and to help prevent link rot. Perma.cc is free for academic libraries and the journals and faculty they support, and it's easy to use and administer.
